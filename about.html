About me

Hello, fellow plant enthusiasts! I'm Elin, and I'm thrilled to welcome you to my green oasis in the digital world. At 37 years young, I've discovered that there's no age limit on falling head over heels for the wonderful world of indoor plants.

What started as a single plant quickly turned into an ever-growing collection, each with its own personality and story. As I nurtured my leafy companions, I realized that they were not just decorations but living, breathing beings that brought life and vibrancy to my home.
'Plantastic Living' is more than just a blog; it's a labor of love. It's my way of sharing my passion for plants, their remarkable qualities, and the joy they bring to everyday life. From transforming my living space into a lush paradise to exploring the countless species and varieties that grace our homes, I'm here to document my journey and share the insights I've gathered along the way.
So, whether you're a seasoned plant parent or just starting your green journey, I invite you to join me in this exciting adventure of 'Plantastic Living.' Let's connect, grow, and explore the beauty of the botanical world together.
